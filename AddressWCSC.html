World Class Skill Centre

Via Metro

1. Take the Blue Line and get off at Tilak Nagar Metro Station (Gate No. 3). After exiting, head towards Jail Road.

2. From there, you can take a short taxi/auto ride or hop on an e-rickshaw (heading towards Delhi Cantt) and ask to be dropped off at ITI Hari Nagar.

Learn More About Blue Line

Via Bus

1. The nearby bus stop is Tihar Jail, and the World Class Skill Centre is just a 5-minute (350 meters) walk from the bus stand.

2. If you're coming from Tilak Nagar Metro and prefer to take a bus, hop on Bus No. 588 or 793 to reach Tihar Jail Bus Stop.
